# TechVerse E-commerce Platform

TechVerse is a comprehensive MERN stack e-commerce platform designed for selling technology products including phones, tablets, computers, TVs, gaming consoles, watches, audio equipment, cameras, accessories, smart home devices, and fitness products.

## Core Features

- **Customer Experience**: User authentication, product search with filters, shopping cart, wishlist, order management, multiple payment methods, address management, and responsive design
- **Admin Management**: Dashboard with analytics, product/inventory management, order processing, user administration, category management, and reporting
- **Technical Capabilities**: RESTful API, real-time updates via WebSocket, Redis caching, JWT authentication, file upload (Cloudinary/AWS S3), comprehensive testing suite

## Product Categories

The platform supports 11 main product categories with sophisticated option structures:
- Each category has color variants and a second option that affects pricing (storage, screen size, configuration, etc.)
- Products include detailed specifications, features, images, reviews, and inventory tracking
- Advanced product search with faceted filtering, autocomplete, and suggestions

## Business Model

B2C e-commerce platform with focus on technology products, featuring:
- Multi-tier pricing based on product configurations
- Inventory management with low stock alerts
- Order lifecycle from placement to delivery tracking
- Customer reviews and ratings system
- Analytics and reporting for business insights
